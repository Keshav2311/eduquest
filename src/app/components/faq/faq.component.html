Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduQuest LMS?

EduQuest LMS is an online platform that helps educators and learners manage, deliver, and track courses efficiently. It's designed to support personalized and engaging learning experiences.

How do I sign up for EduQuest LMS?

Click the Sign Up button, fill in your details, and verify your email. Once done, you can log in and start exploring courses immediately.

What types of courses are available?

EduQuest offers courses on academic subjects, professional skills, certifications, and hobbies like photography and writing, catering to students and professionals alike.

Can I use EduQuest LMS on my phone?

Yes, EduQuest is mobile-friendly and has an app for iOS and Android. You can also download course materials for offline access.

How do I track my learning progress?

Your dashboard displays progress bars, completed lessons, and quiz scores, helping you stay on top of your goals and certifications.
